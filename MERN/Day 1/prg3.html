Our Departments

Computer Science

The Computer Science Department offers a variety of courses in programming, algorithms, and data structures.

Key Areas: Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering Department focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.

Key Areas: Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Machine Design

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering Department specializes in electrical systems, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Key Areas: Power Systems, Control Systems, Signal Processing

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering Department is dedicated to the design and construction of infrastructure projects.

Key Areas: Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering
